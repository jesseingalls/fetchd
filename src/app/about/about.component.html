Our Story... In my years as a touring musician, I was always scrambling to get everything together I needed for my trips -> Drives to the pharmacy for toiletries and healthy snacks, trips to the department store for basics like shirts and undies, not to mention work-out clothes and gig/evening atire. Then having to lug a giant suitcase to the airport...only to pay $25-$50 a bag.

If your a parent like me, then you know how hard it is to pack all your items like diapers, snacks, and bottles.

What if there was a way to take away all that hassel and stress, and save some money at the same time. In a way that was helping with environmental sustainability?

With Fetch'd TraveLight, travelers can have all their custom items at their hotel upon arrival, packed nicely in their Fetch'd duffel bag.

After your trip you can either fly home with your new duffel or easily return it, and many clothing items to us.

With our 'Green Initiative', and 'Used but New' clothing options, we aim to reduce carbon imprint as well.
